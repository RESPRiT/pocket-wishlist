When Used

[edit | edit source]
You present your charter to the ticket lady at Elemental International Airport (near the Right Side of the Tracks in Seaside Town.) Your name gets added to the registry of wealthy Adventurers who are allowed to go to Spring Break Beach whenever they want to.
