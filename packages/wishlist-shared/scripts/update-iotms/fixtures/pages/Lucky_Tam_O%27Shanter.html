lucky Tam O'Shanter

This is some kind of beret with a fuzzy ball on the top of it. Your familiar can wear it at a jaunty angle. Then you could say "Jaunty Tam O'Shanter" over and over again. Try it, it's fun!

Type: familiar equipment

Notes

[edit | edit source]
  • March 2005's item of the month from Mr. Store.
Its in-store description, unfortunately, has been lost to the sands of time.
  • Cannot be equipped on a Comma Chameleon.
  • This item's enchantment used to be +10 lbs. of Leprechaun.
  • Mr Skullhead was unaware when he created this item that the Tam O'Shanter is Scottish and not Irish and not, perhaps, the best choice of hat for a leprechaun.
  • First non-familiar Item Of The Month.
  • There exists an alternate version of this item.
  • First prize in The Raffle House on February 12, 2008.
