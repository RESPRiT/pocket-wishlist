Scratch 'n' Sniff Sticker Tome

The pages of this ancient tome contain an infinitely-replenishing supply of stickers. And as if this wasn't magical enough, the stickers themselves smell like stuff when you scratch them!

When Used

[edit | edit source]
  • Without a mystical bookshelf:
You take the Tome to your campsite, and a strange shimmering bookshelf appears near your dwelling. You put the Tome on it.
  • With a mystical bookshelf without a Scratch 'n' Sniff Sticker Tome on it:
You carefully place the Tome on the bookshelf in your campsite.
  • With a Scratch 'n' Sniff Sticker Tome already on the mystical bookshelf:
You've already got a copy of that Tome on your bookshelf.

Notes

[edit | edit source]
  • November 2008's item of the month from Mr. Store.
Its in-store description: Contains a lot of stickers that you can use to make a groovy customized weapon.
  • When placed on a player's Mystical Bookshelf, this item grants use of the skill Summon Stickers. This skill can be used in all Normal and Hardcore ascensions thereafter.
