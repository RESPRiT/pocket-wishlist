Grumpy Bumpkin's Pumpkin Seed Catalog

This is a catalog of various varieties of pumpkin seed, like "that one that's a little pointier than t'others," and "that there one what grows into a pumpkin! Now leave me alone!" Clearly, the catalog's rural author was not in the best of moods when he wrote it. Still, it's worth reading for the tagline on the back:

"Enjoy Grumpy Bumpkin's Lumpy Pumpkins."

Gives this item when used:


packet of pumpkin seeds

When Used

  • Without A Pumpkin Patch in your campground:

Pumpkin patches are like soul patches -- you can't have more than one of them. No sense in ordering more seeds.
