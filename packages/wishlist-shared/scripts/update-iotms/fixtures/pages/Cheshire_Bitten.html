Cheshire Bitten

This is a baby Cheshire Bat. No arguments - if a baby cat is a kitten, a baby bat is a bitten. Anyway, Cheshire Bats are known for their mysterious smile and their even more mysterious habit of disappearing and re-appearing with large amounts of meat.
