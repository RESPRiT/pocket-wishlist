container of Spooky Putty

This is a little egg-shaped container of Spooky Putty, the moldable, squeezable, ink-transferring, make-your-blood-run-cold-in-your-veins toy that all the goth kids adore.

This item can be squished into the following forms:


Spooky Putty mitre



Spooky Putty leotard



Spooky Putty ball



Spooky Putty sheet



Spooky Putty snake



Cannot be discarded
