Tome of Snowcone Summoning

It is by will alone you summon snowcones. It is by the incantations contained in this tome that the snow acquires form, the form acquires flavoring, the flavoring becomes a delicious treat. It is by will alone you summon snowcones.

When Used

[edit | edit source]
  • Without a mystical bookshelf:
You take the Tome to your campsite, and a strange shimmering bookshelf appears near your dwelling. You put the Tome on it.
  • With a mystical bookshelf without a Tome of Snowcone Summoning on it:
You place the Tome carefully on the bookshelf in your campsite.
  • With a Tome of Snowcone Summoning already on the mystical bookshelf:
You've already got a copy of that Tome on your bookshelf.

Notes

[edit | edit source]
  • January 2006's item of the month from Mr. Store.
Its in-store description: When used, gives you a skill that lets you summon three delicious snowcones per day. Six exciting flavors!
  • When placed on a player's Mystical Bookshelf, this item grants use of the skill Summon Snowcones. This skill can be used in all Normal and Hardcore ascensions thereafter.
  • Fortunately, the Tome does not let you summon the yellow snowcone.
  • According to an Announcement on February 21, 2006, "Tomes of snowcone summoning now mark you no-delete."
  • This was the first Mr. Store item to grant you a skill.
  • First prize in The Raffle House on January 30, 2008.

References

[edit | edit source]
  • The description imitates the form of the Mentat Mantra from David Lynch's movie adaptation of Frank Herbert's novel Dune: "It is by will alone I set my mind in motion. It is by the juice of Sapho that thoughts acquire speed, the lips acquire stains, the stains become a warning. It is by will alone I set my mind in motion."
