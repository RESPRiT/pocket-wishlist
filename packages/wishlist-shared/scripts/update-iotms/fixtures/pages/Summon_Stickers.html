Notes

[edit | edit source]
  • This skill is granted by your mystical bookshelf once you have used the Tome, and thus it is available anytime you have access to your bookshelf (including Hardcore, but not runs such as Bad Moon or Zombie Slayer).
  • Each Tome's summons can be used up to three times each day.
    • During Ronin and Hardcore you may only summon three times from all your Tomes in total each day.
    • After ascending, the counter on Tomes is reset and you can use them another three times each.
  • Three random stickers are created each time the skill is used, for a maximum of nine stickers per day.
  • These stickers grant a bonus to your scratch 'n' sniff sword/crossbow. Up to three can be stuck to the weapon at a time, and duplicate effects stack.
  • Each sticker lasts for 20 combats with the weapon equipped. At the end of its 20th combat, a sticker will disappear with the message:
A sticker falls off your weapon, faded and torn.
  • Free runaways do wear off stickers.
  • Adventures in areas such as The Briny Deeps, where combats normally consume 2 adventures, do not wear out stickers faster than normal combats.
  • When viewing the stickers on your scratch 'n' sniff weapon, each sticker will be described as "shiny" if it's relatively new and as "dull" if it's close to wearing out.
