Notes

[edit | edit source]
  • Is automatically placed in your inventory for you at the beginning of your run, with the message:
    Ironically, you dig your Archaeologist's Spade out of the ether without your spade.
    You acquire an item: Archaeologist's Spade
  • Has a [dig] link that takes you to Dig at Location. Unless there are no digs remaining in which case shows the message:
    You've run out of digging energy for the day. Archaeology is hard work!
  • Trying to dig if your last adventure was not at a valid adventuring location returns the message:
    You don't have anywhere in particular to dig right now. Try adventuring somewhere else.
