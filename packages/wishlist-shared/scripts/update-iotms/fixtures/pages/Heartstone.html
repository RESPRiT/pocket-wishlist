Heartstone

A gemstone shaped like a cartoon heart, which pulses in time with your own heart, which is shaped like a weird veiny lump.

Type: accessory
Cannot be traded or discarded

All Attributes +25%
+3 Stat(s) Per Fight
Regenerate 15-20 HP per adventure
Steal the Hearts of Foes


NOTE: You may not equip more than one of these at a time.

Notes

[edit | edit source]
  • Is automatically placed in your inventory for you at the beginning of your run, with the message:
    Home is where your heartstone is, and your heartstone is now at home in your inventory.
    You acquire an item: Heartstone
  • Grants the skill Steal Monster's Heart.
  • Has additional skills that must be permanently unlocked via Steal Monster's Heart. Afterwards they are conditional on the Heartstone being equipped.
    • Combat skills:
      • Heartstone: BOOM (5/day, Insta-kill with Stat gains)
      • Heartstone: GONE (5/day, Insta-kill Banish)
      • Heartstone: STUN (5/day, Multi-round Stun)
    • Noncombat skills:
      • Heartstone: BUDS (5/day, Best Pals (25 turns))
      • Heartstone: BUFF (5/day, Ultraheart (25 turns))
      • Heartstone: LUCK (1/day, Lucky! intrinsic)
  • Has a [use] link that takes you to the campground.
  • Can be placed in The Eternity Codpiece, which continues to allow access to the skills when worn.

References

[edit | edit source]
  • The [use] link taking you to your campsite is a reference to the World of Warcraft Hearthstone items that teleport the player back to their current lodgings.
    • Amusingly, if one were to use Steal Monster's Heart to extract the middle letter of "Hearthstone", one would be left with the name of this item.
