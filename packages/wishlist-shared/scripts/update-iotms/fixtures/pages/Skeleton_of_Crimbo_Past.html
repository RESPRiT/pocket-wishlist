Skeleton of Crimbo Past

Skeletal merchant --
He's got some rare things on sale,
stranger. Ho ho ho!

Increases item drops from monsters
Sells artifacts of Crimbos gone by... but not for meat...

Ability: Increases item drops as a 1x fairy. Drops up to 100 knucklebones per day, which are spent in Visiting the Skeleton of Crimbo Past. Knucklebones drop when resting at your campground (5 per day) and in combat.

Throne/Bjorn: +100% Damage vs. Skeletons

Hatchling: stocking full of bones

Familiar-Specific Equipment: small peppermint-flavored sugar walking crook
