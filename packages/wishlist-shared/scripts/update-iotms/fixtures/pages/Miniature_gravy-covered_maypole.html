miniature gravy-covered maypole

This is a maypole that's covered from tip to bottom in delicious gravy. From the top stream several ribbons, each of which is also covered in delicious gravy. A familiar using this thing would probably be able to find a lot more items than usual after combat. Why does gravy help your familiar find more items? Exactly.

Type: familiar equipment
Familiar: any
Cannot be discarded

+25% Item Drops from Monsters
