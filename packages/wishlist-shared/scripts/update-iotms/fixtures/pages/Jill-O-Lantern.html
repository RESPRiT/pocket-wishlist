Notes

[edit | edit source]
  • Was available in the Mr. Store during October, 2004.
  • Damage done is typically two portions: One portion that is non-elemental, and a second portion that is either hot (60% of the time) or spooky (40% of the time).
  • Does from int((wt+2)/2) to wt+2 in damage, split evenly between physical and elemental damage. Extra points go to elemental damage.
  • Attacks 25% of the time, regardless of weight.
  • Grants stat gains based on the volleyball formula: SquareRoot(Weight).

References

[edit | edit source]
  • The first line of the description, "Hail the pumpkin queen", is a play on "Everyone hail to the Pumpkin King", an emphasized lyric of the song "This Is Halloween" from the movie The Nightmare Before Christmas.
  • It is also possibly a play on the character names from "Jack and Jill": a Jack-O-Lantern/Jill-O-Lantern.
