When Used

[edit | edit source]
  • From inventory, by clicking [tune moon], if you've already done that this ascension:

You can't figure out the angle to see the moon's reflection in the spoon anymore.


  • From inventory, by clicking [tune moon] the first time this ascension:

You notice that the reflection of the moon looks different in the bowl of the spoon.

{moon sign menu}

Change Moon Sign!

You feel like this is not something you'll be able to do again, at least not in this life.


  • After selecting a moon sign and clicking Change Moon Sign!:

You twist the spoon around until the reflection of the moon in the bowl looks just like you intended.

  • Attempting to switch away from Bad Moon in Bad Moon's "aftercore":

This moon is too bad to change.

Notes

[edit | edit source]
  • Grants one of the following combat skills, depending on your class' mainstat:
    • Dragoon Platoon for Muscle classes
    • Spittoon Monsoon for Mysticality classes
    • Festoon Buffoon for Moxie classes
  • In combat, randomly replaces various words by another that rhymes, and puts them in italic.
  • Sometimes drop a cartoon harpoon after combat.
  • Is automatically placed in your inventory for you at the beginning of your run, with the message:
You are reborn with a hewn moon-run spoon in your mouth.
You acquire an item: hewn moon-rune spoon
