Notes

[edit | edit source]
  • Is automatically placed in your inventory for you at the beginning of your run, with the message:
    They say some are born with a silver spoon in their mouth, but you've been reborn with a pasta wand in your hand.
    You acquire an item: legendary pasta wand
  • The "Very Accurate!" enchantment gives a +1000 bonus to Hit Chance.
  • Allows you to summon legendary noodles. If you have the Pastamastery skill, the legendary noodles are summoned alongside your dry noodles. If you lack the Pastamastery skill, the pasta wand grants the Wave your Pasta Wand skill to summon standalone legendary noodles.
    • If the pasta wand is in your Closet, then legendary noodles will not be summoned.
    • See the legendary noodles page for cooking recipes.
  • Pasta Thralls gain new abilities at Level 11, and non-Pastamancer thrall buffs are tripled:
