Notes

[edit | edit source]
  • This skill is granted by your mystical bookshelf once you have used the Tome, and thus it is available anytime you have access to your bookshelf (including Hardcore, but not runs such as Bad Moon or Zombie Slayer).
  • Each Tome's summons can be used up to three times each day.
    • During Ronin and Hardcore you may only summon three times from all your Tomes in total each day.
    • After ascending, the counter on Tomes is reset and you can use them another three times each.
  • Each snowcone's effect lasts for 20 turns. You may only have one Tongue effect active at once.
  • On January 3, 2008, this skill was no longer attainable. The functionality was instead moved to the mystical bookshelf.
  • The skill used to summon three snowcones at once, for 5 MP, and was usable once per day. Rarely, it would summon a black snowcone instead of three regular snowcones, with the message: "You mess up the incantation, and instead of summoning three snowcones, you end up with one big mixed-up one."
  • Black snowcone rate is being spaded.
