When Used

[edit | edit source]
  • With a bottle already used:
    You already see lovebugs wherever you go. There's no need to attract more.
  • Otherwise:
    Why do bugs suddenly appear, every time you are near? Because you just slathered yourself up with bug pheromones, that's why.

    Lovebug abilities and encounters have been permanently unlocked. Watch out!

Notes

[edit | edit source]
  • February 2015's item of the month from Mr. Store.
Its in-store description: Attracts helpful bugs to do your bidding and bring you gifts.
  • The skills are granted and persist regardless of ascension path, including Picky and Actually Ed the Undying.

References

[edit | edit source]
  • The use text is a reference to the song "(They Long to Be) Close to You", a Bacharach/David song most famously covered by The Carpenters.
