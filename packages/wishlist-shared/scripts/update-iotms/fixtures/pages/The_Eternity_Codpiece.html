Notes

[edit | edit source]
  • Is automatically placed in your inventory for you at the beginning of your run, with the message:
    Whoa, whoa, whoa. You nearly forgot your codpiece. And your codpiece is eternal!
  • Has a [decorate] link.
    • Takes you to Decorate Your Eternity Codpiece.
    • Link chat macro: /goto inventory.php?pwd='+pwdhash+'&action=docodpiece
  • Gems placed in The Eternity Codpiece grant bonuses. See "Decorate Your Eternity Codpiece" for the list.
    • Gems can be freely added and removed, and persist across the Astral Gash and into your next ascension.
    • If a gem that grants a skill is placed in the codpiece, that skill can still be used (e.g., Peridot of Peril, blood cubic zirconia, Heartstone, Baseball Diamond). However, you don't get the regular enchantment bonuses of those gems -- only their skills, and the Codpiece's enchantment bonuses for them.
    • Standard restrictions have no bearing on the attachment or detachment of gems. You may attach any gem you have, including restricted ones, while on any ascension path.
  • According to Jick, the cursed black pearl union was not included because they "discussed it but concluded that people probably didn't want stinky onions near their crotches."
